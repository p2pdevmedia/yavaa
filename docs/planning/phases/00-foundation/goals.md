# Goals

## Phase
Foundation / Infrastructure

## Product goals
- Establish stable project architecture and tooling baseline.
- Ensure auth, API-first contracts, CI/CD, and testing infrastructure are operational.
- Provide logging/audit foundation and deterministic seed workflow.

## Non-goals
- Avoid premature over-engineering.
- Avoid hidden state and non-deterministic flows.
- Avoid bypassing server-side permissions.
